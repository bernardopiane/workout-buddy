# Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Workout Buddy** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal
information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose,
and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy
policy, please do not access the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the
App includes:

### Personal Data

Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, and authentication
credentials, that you voluntarily give to us when you register with the App.

### Workout and Health Data

We collect data related to your workouts, including exercises performed, sets, repetitions, weights,
and dates. This data is essential for the App's core functionality of tracking your fitness
progress.

### Usage Data

We may collect information about how you access and use the App, such as your device type, operating
system, and usage patterns.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
- Track and display your workout history and progress.
- Improve user experience and the quality of our App.
- Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests.

## 4. Storage and Data Security

### Backend Services

We use **Supabase** as our backend service provider for authentication and data storage. By using
our App, you acknowledge that your data may be stored on Supabase servers. You can review Supabase's
Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

### Local Storage

We use local storage on your device (e.g., Shared Preferences) to store certain settings and cached
data to improve the App's performance and offline capabilities.

### Security

We implement reasonable security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.
However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of
data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may share information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including
data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, customer service, and marketing assistance.

- **Supabase**: Backend and Database services.

## 6. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

- **Access**: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
- **Correction**: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is
  inaccurate.
- **Deletion**: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain
  conditions.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the contact information provided below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect
personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have
collected personal data from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete that
information.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the
new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any
